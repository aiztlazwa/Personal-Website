Brassband & Colorguard

Participating in the brassband and color guard at university has been an enriching experience that allowed me to gain valuable knowledge and skills. I learned to play a musical instrument from scratch, mastering techniques and understanding the intricacies of reading and interpreting music notes. Beyond music, I developed proficiency in color guard performance, particularly the art of using flags to convey emotion and enhance visual storytelling during routines. These activities not only honed my technical abilities but also strengthened my discipline, teamwork, and creativity.
